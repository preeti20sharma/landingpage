About us

Ireo The Corridors

Ireo The Corridors, located in Sector-67A Gurgaon, is one of the city’s largest condominiums spread across 37.5 acres. With 10 acres of landscaped greens, fully-furnished apartments, and modern amenities like a clubhouse, swimming pool, kids play area, sports arenas, school, hospital, and retail zones – it offers a complete self-contained lifestyle. Ready-to-move-in homes here provide natural light, ventilation, and stunning views, making it perfect for families of all sizes. Well-connected to Gurgaon Railway Station and IGI Airport, The Corridors ensures comfort, safety, and luxury all in one community.

About Developer

IREO is a leading pan-India developer and the first and largest foreign direct investment (FDI) investor in Indian property. This key real estate developer has invested US $1.5 billion in building a portfolio of high quality executable projects in prominent locations across 3,000 acres of owned land – one of the leading pan-India asset portfolios. IREO has evolved as a fully integrated real estate organization that is the developer of its projects, and possesses a diversified portfolio.

IREO is developing a portfolio of master-planned communities on approximately 800 acres of land in Gurgaon region.

Since 2003, IREO has been working hard to make a mark in improving the nation's infrastructure.
